Frequently Asked Questions

What is Musical Passport?

Musical Passport is a music discovery app that helps you explore music from countries around the world. Pick any country, and we'll surface artists and tracks you probably haven't heard before - verified on streaming platforms and enriched with cultural context.

Which streaming services are supported?

Musical Passport supports Apple Music, Spotify, and Deezer. You can open any track directly in your preferred streaming app. Preview clips are also available for many tracks within the app itself.

How do I save songs I like?

Tap the heart icon on any track to save it to your Liked Songs. You can view all your saved tracks by tapping the heart button in the floating navigation bar. To unlike a song, swipe right on it in your Liked Songs list.

Why can't I find a track on my streaming service?

Streaming availability varies by country and catalog licensing. Some tracks - especially from smaller regions or older recordings - may be on one platform but not another. Try tapping the three-dot menu on a track and choosing a different service.

If a track is consistently missing or incorrectly matched, please email us and we'll look into it.

How does the Genre Spotlight work?

Tapping a genre tag on any artist opens the Genre Spotlight - a curated view of artists and tracks representing that genre from the same country. It's a great way to go deeper into a sound you've discovered.

A track or artist seems wrong for that country. How do I report it?

Tap the three-dot (…) menu next to any track, then select Flag for Review (available to beta testers). Alternatively, email us at musicalpassportapp@gmail.com with the artist name, track title, and country - we'll investigate and fix it promptly.

My data or saved songs disappeared. What happened?

Liked songs and passport stamps are stored locally on your device. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, this data will not transfer automatically. We're working on cloud sync for a future update. If something disappeared unexpectedly without a reinstall, please contact us.

How do I delete my data?

Musical Passport does not create user accounts or store personal data on our servers. All your saved songs and stamps live on your device. To delete everything, simply uninstall the app. For any questions about data, see our Privacy Policy or email us.
